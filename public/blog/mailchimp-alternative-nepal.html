The best Mailchimp alternative for Nepal

Updated June 2026 · 6 min read

Mailchimp is a fine tool — built for marketers in the US, priced in dollars. If you run email from Nepal, here's why a Nepal-first platform fits better, and how to switch without losing anything.

Why Nepali businesses look for a Mailchimp alternative

Most teams in Nepal hit the same three walls with global tools like Mailchimp and MailerLite:

  • You can't pay easily. Billing is in USD and needs an international card. No eSewa, no Khalti, no NPR invoice for your accountant.
  • One brand per account. If you're an agency running email for several clients, you're juggling a separate login and a separate bill for each.
  • It's priced for the US. Dollar pricing on a Nepali budget adds up fast — especially across multiple client accounts.

What a Nepal-first platform does differently

SendMyMail is built around how Nepali agencies and brands actually work:

  • NPR billing with eSewa, Khalti and IME Pay — and PAN-backed VAT invoices.
  • Many clients in one workspace — switch between brands in a click, each with its own contacts, branding and reports.
  • Per-client brand kits — every section you drop lands already on-brand.
  • Deliverability tuned for Nepal — landing in the Gmail and Outlook inboxes your recipients use.
  • Local support in your timezone.
You don't have to choose between “powerful” and “local.” The point of a Nepal-first tool is both.

“But I have years of data in Mailchimp”

That's the usual blocker — and it's smaller than it feels. Migrating takes an afternoon:

  1. Export your contacts from Mailchimp as a CSV and import them — lists and tags come along.
  2. Rebuild your core templates with the drag-and-drop editor (most teams have only a handful that matter).
  3. Verify your sending domain — we generate the SPF/DKIM/DMARC records to paste into DNS.
  4. Send a test campaign, confirm inbox placement, and you're live.

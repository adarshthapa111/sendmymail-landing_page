Top questions this week

How long does SPF & DKIM verification take after I update DNS?
Usually 15 minutes to 4 hours, occasionally up to 24. Once your DNS provider publishes the new records, hit "Re-check" on the domain page — we verify in real time. NTC and Worldlink propagate fastest in Nepal; some smaller ISPs cache longer.
Can I import a contact list I bought from a vendor?
No, and we'll actively block it. Lists with > 10% role accounts (info@, admin@) or scraped patterns are rejected at import. Beyond our terms, sending to unconsented lists violates Nepal's Advertisement Act 2076 and tanks your sender reputation across Gmail / Outlook.
Why are my open rates lower in the report than I expected?
Apple Mail Privacy Protection masks opens — if > 40% of your list reads on iPhone, true open rate is higher than reported. Use clicks and revenue as the trustier signals.
How does billing in NPR work with eSewa / Khalti?
We auto-charge your saved eSewa or Khalti account on your billing date. Invoices include your PAN and VAT line items so they're audit-ready. You can switch payment methods anytime from the Billing page.
Can my client log in to see only their own reports?
Yes — invite them as a Viewer scoped to just their client from the Team page. They'll see reports for that one client and nothing else.
What happens when my 14-day trial ends?
Sending pauses until you add a payment method. Your contacts, templates, and campaigns stay safe — nothing is deleted for 90 days.
